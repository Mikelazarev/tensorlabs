TensorLabs is a startup uniting experts in medicine, AI, and engineering to create technologies that truly improve people's lives.
Our goal is to turn recent scientific breakthroughs into practical solutions for early diagnosis and health monitoring.
Why is this needed?
Cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of death worldwide.
Early diagnosis can save thousands of lives, but modern medicine does not always offer fast and accessible solutions.
Ischemic heart disease (IHD) remains the number one cause of death and long-term disability due to cardiovascular diseases globally. Accurate, simple-to-use, and cost-effective screening methods for IHD are in high demand.
Our approach: we use computer vision and AI to diagnose based on accessible images. We detect features, assess risk, refer, and support the patient with postoperative monitoring.
Current Projects
IHD Detection from Face Photo
Diagnosis of ischemic heart disease based on analysis of patient face images.
Data collection in progress, first results obtained, publications for scientific journals are being prepared
IHD Detection from Fundus Photo
Computer analysis of fundus images for non-invasive assessment of cardiovascular risk.
Database expansion and publication of results planned
The Medical Device of the Future
AI assistant for doctors, for postoperative health monitoring of patients.
Language model development in progress
